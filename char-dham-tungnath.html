About Char Dham Yatra With Tungnath

Location: Uttarakhand, India
Trek Duration: 10 DAYS / 9 NIGHTS
Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult
Trekking distance: 40 Km
Best Time: April to June
Age limit: 12 to 60 Years

Char Dham of Uttarakhand is one of the most revered Hindu Pilgrimages in India where thousands of people visit every year to seek spirituality within. Experience this through this extensive package from Haridwar and get close to the enchanting majesty of Hinduism. The Char Dham circuit comprises of four holy sites - Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. Along with these sacred temples, this package also includes a visit to Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world, offering breathtaking views and spiritual tranquility.

Day 1: Arrival | Haridwar to Kharsali via Mussoorie

Begin your spiritual journey from Haridwar. Travel through the scenic hill station of Mussoorie and reach Kharsali, the base for Yamunotri temple.

Day 2: Barkot – Yamunotri

Trek or take a pony ride to Yamunotri temple, the source of river Yamuna. Seek blessings at this sacred shrine nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas.

Day 3: Barkot to Uttarkashi

Drive to Uttarkashi, a holy town on the banks of river Bhagirathi. Visit the famous Vishwanath Temple and explore the spiritual atmosphere.

Day 4: Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi

Visit Gangotri temple, the origin of the holy river Ganga. Experience the divine energy and pristine beauty of this sacred place.

Day 5: Uttarkashi – Phata

Journey towards Phata, the base for Kedarnath. Prepare for the trek to one of the most revered Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.

Day 6: Phata – Gaurikund – Kedarnath

Trek to Kedarnath temple at 11,755 ft. Witness the majestic shrine surrounded by snow-capped peaks and seek divine blessings.

Day 7: Kedarnath – Bari Village

Descend from Kedarnath and travel to Bari Village. Rest and rejuvenate after the spiritual trek.

Day 8: Chopta – Gopeshwar

Visit Chopta, the "Mini Switzerland of India". Trek to Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world at 12,073 ft, and continue to Chandrashila peak for panoramic Himalayan views.

Day 9: Gopeshwar – Badrinath

Journey to Badrinath, one of the four sacred Char Dham sites. Visit the ancient Badrinath temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and experience the spiritual aura.

Day 10: Joshimath – Haridwar

Return journey to Haridwar with divine blessings and unforgettable memories of the complete Char Dham Yatra with Tungnath.
