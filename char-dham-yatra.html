About Char Dham Yatra

Location: Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand
Trek Duration: 09 DAYS / 08 NIGHTS
Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult
Trekking distance: 40 Km
Age limit: 12 to 60 Years

Char Dham Yatra is one of the most important Hindu Pilgrimages in India where thousands of people visit every year to seek spirituality within. Experience this through this extensive package from Haridwar and get close to the enchanting prayers of Hinduism.

Day 0: ARRIVAL | HARIDWAR TO BARKOT VIA MUSSOORIE

Begin your spiritual journey from Haridwar. Drive through the scenic route via Mussoorie to reach Barkot.

Day 1: BARKOT – YAMUNOTRI

Visit the sacred Yamunotri temple, the source of river Yamuna. Experience divine blessings at this holy shrine.

Day 2: BARKOT – UTTARKASHI

Journey to Uttarkashi, a town known for its spiritual significance and beautiful Himalayan views.

Day 3: UTTARKASHI – GANGOTRI – UTTARKASHI

Visit Gangotri temple, the origin of holy river Ganga. Witness the divine beauty of this sacred place.

Day 4: UTTARKASHI – PHATA

Travel to Phata, the base for Kedarnath temple visit. Prepare for the spiritual journey ahead.

Day 5: PHATA – GAURIKUND – KEDARNATH

Trek or take helicopter to Kedarnath, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Experience divine spirituality.

Day 6: KEDARNATH – PHATA

After morning prayers at Kedarnath, return to Phata. Carry the divine blessings with you.

Day 7: PHATA – BADRINATH

Visit Badrinath temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Complete your Char Dham pilgrimage.

Day 8: BADRINATH – HARIDWAR

Return journey to Haridwar with memories of divine darshan and spiritual enlightenment.
