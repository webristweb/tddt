About Hampta Pass Trek With Chandratal

Region: Himachal Pradesh
Duration: 5 days / 4 Nights
Grade: Easy

Hampta Pass is a breathtaking mountain pass in the Pir Panjal range, connecting Manali's lush Kullu Valley to the stark landscapes of Spiti. At an altitude of around 14,100 feet, it offers a dramatic contrast of green valleys, arid barren mountains, and pristine glaciers. The trek to Chandratal Lake, also known as the "Moon Lake," adds a magical touch to this adventure. Witness the crystal-clear turquoise waters of Chandratal surrounded by towering peaks, creating an unforgettable Himalayan experience.

Day 1: MANALI ARRIVAL DRIVE TO JOBRI TREK TO CHIKA

Arrive in Manali, the gateway to the Himalayas. Drive to Jobri and begin your trek through lush green forests and meadows to reach Chika campsite. Enjoy the serene mountain atmosphere and prepare for the adventure ahead.

Day 2: CHIKA TO BALU KA GERA TREK

Trek through beautiful alpine meadows and cross crystal-clear mountain streams. Reach Balu Ka Gera, a stunning campsite surrounded by towering peaks. Experience the raw beauty of the Himalayas as you camp under the stars.

Day 3: BALU KA GERA TO SHEAGORU TREK VIA HAMPTA PASS

The most thrilling day of the trek! Cross the magnificent Hampta Pass at 14,100 feet. Witness the dramatic landscape change from green valleys to barren mountains. Descend to Sheagoru and experience the stark beauty of the Spiti side.

Day 4: SHEAGORU TO CHATTRU TREK CHATTRU TO CHANDRATAL DRIVE AND BACK

Trek down to Chattru and drive to the mesmerizing Chandratal Lake, the "Moon Lake" at 14,100 feet. Marvel at the turquoise waters surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Spend time at this pristine high-altitude lake before returning to Chattru.

Day 5: DRIVE FROM CHATTRU TO MANALI TOUR ENDS

Drive back to Manali through scenic mountain roads, crossing the Rohtang Pass. Reflect on the incredible journey through diverse landscapes, from lush valleys to barren mountains and pristine lakes. Tour concludes with unforgettable memories.
