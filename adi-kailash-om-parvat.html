About Adi Kailash & Om Parvat

Location: Uttarakhand
Trek Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights
Difficulty Level: Easy to Moderate

Embark on a mystical pilgrimage to Adi Kailash and Om Parvat, two sacred destinations nestled in the breathtaking landscape of the Indian Himalayas. Often referred to as the 'Chota Kailash' or 'Little Kailash,' Adi Kailash is believed to be the original abode of Lord Shiva. Marvel at the awe-inspiring sight of Om Parvat, where the natural formation of the sacred 'Om' symbol on the mountainside is a testament to the divine majesty of the Himalayas.

Experience the spiritual serenity and pristine beauty of these sacred sites on our transformative journey designed to nourish the body, mind, and soul.

DAY 1: KATHGODAM - PITHORAGARH

Begin your spiritual journey from Kathgodam railway station. Drive through scenic mountain roads, passing through beautiful valleys and traditional villages. Reach Pithoragarh, a picturesque town nestled in the Kumaon Himalayas. Explore the local market and prepare for the sacred pilgrimage ahead. Overnight stay in Pithoragarh.

DAY 2: PITHORAGARH - DHARCHULA

Drive from Pithoragarh to Dharchula, a border town on the banks of Kali River. The journey offers stunning views of the Panchachuli peaks and lush green valleys. Dharchula serves as the gateway to the Indo-Tibet border region. Complete necessary permits and documentation. Overnight stay in Dharchula.

DAY 3: DHARCHULA - GUNJI

Journey through the rugged terrain along the Indo-Tibet border. Drive through remote villages and witness the dramatic landscape change. Cross several high-altitude passes with breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks. Reach Gunji, a small village at high altitude. Acclimatize to the altitude and rest. Overnight stay in Gunji.

DAY 4: GUNJI - ADI KAILASH - GUNJI

The most sacred day of the pilgrimage! Drive to Adi Kailash (Chota Kailash), believed to be the original abode of Lord Shiva. Witness the majestic pyramid-shaped peak resembling Mount Kailash in Tibet. Perform parikrama (circumambulation) and seek divine blessings. Experience the spiritual energy of this sacred site. Return to Gunji for overnight stay.

DAY 5: GUNJI - OM PARVAT - GUNJI / NABHI

Early morning drive to witness the divine Om Parvat. Marvel at the natural formation of the sacred 'Om' symbol (ॐ) on the snow-covered mountainside - a miraculous sight that appears only during certain times of the year. This divine manifestation is considered a blessing from Lord Shiva. Spend time in meditation and photography. Return to Gunji or proceed to Nabhi for overnight stay.

DAY 6: GUNJI / NABHI - DHARCHULA - DIDHAT

Begin the return journey from the high-altitude regions. Drive back through the scenic mountain roads to Dharchula and continue to Didhat. Reflect on the spiritual experiences and divine darshan of Adi Kailash and Om Parvat. Overnight stay in Didhat.

DAY 7: DIDHAT - KATHGODAM DROP

Drive back to Kathgodam railway station. Conclude your sacred pilgrimage with divine blessings and unforgettable memories of Adi Kailash and the miraculous Om Parvat. Depart with a spiritually enriched soul and renewed faith.
