About Udaipur With Kumbhalgarh

Location: Rajasthan, India
Tour Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights
Type: Cultural & Heritage Tour

Udaipur is Rajasthan's crown jewel, a city of shimmering lakes, grand palaces, and vibrant bazaars. Often hailed as the "Venice of the East," Udaipur is a vibrant masterpiece of royal history and serene waters nestled in the heart of Rajasthan. Their stay back in time at Kumbhalgarh, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famed for its massive fort walls stretching over 36 km and encircling the Aravalli hills, a perfect blend of history, culture, and scenic beauty.

Day 0: DEPART FROM DELHI/GURUGRAM - OVERNIGHT JOURNEY

Begin your royal Rajasthan adventure with an overnight journey from Delhi/Gurugram. Travel comfortably as you head towards the City of Lakes, Udaipur. Get ready to experience the grandeur of Rajasthan's rich heritage and culture.

Day 1: REACH UDAIPUR | LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

Arrive in the enchanting city of Udaipur. Explore the magnificent City Palace overlooking Lake Pichola, visit the beautiful Jagdish Temple, and stroll through the vibrant local markets. Enjoy a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola and witness the stunning sunset views of the Lake Palace and Jag Mandir.

Day 2: FATEH SAGAR LAKE | MONSOON PALACE | BAGUBALI HILLS

Visit the picturesque Fateh Sagar Lake, an artificial lake surrounded by hills and parks. Explore the majestic Monsoon Palace (Sajjangarh Palace) perched on a hilltop, offering panoramic views of the city and surrounding countryside. Trek to Bagubali Hills for breathtaking sunset views and capture stunning photographs of Udaipur's landscape.

Day 3: KUMBHALGARH FORT | DEPART FOR DELHI

Journey to the magnificent Kumbhalgarh Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Marvel at the second-longest continuous wall in the world (after the Great Wall of China), stretching over 36 kilometers. Explore the fort's palaces, temples, and gardens while learning about its rich history. Begin your return journey to Delhi with unforgettable memories of royal Rajasthan.

Day 4: REACH DELHI BY MORNING 06.00 AM (TENTATIVELY)

Arrive back in Delhi early morning with cherished memories of Udaipur's lakes, palaces, and the grandeur of Kumbhalgarh Fort. Tour concludes with a perfect blend of culture, history, and scenic beauty.
