About Mcleodganj Triund

Location: Himachal Pradesh
Trek Duration: 3 Days 2 Nights
Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Triund, a jewel crown ridge in the Dhauladhar range. Trek through lush forests, witness panoramic views of the Kangra Valley, and camp under the stars at one of the most scenic locations in Himachal Pradesh. Explore the vibrant Tibetan culture of Mcleodganj, home to His Holiness the Dalai Lama.

Day 0: Departure from Akshardham/Majnu ka tila & Chandigarh Overnight Journey

Begin your adventure with an overnight journey from Delhi/Chandigarh to Mcleodganj. Get ready for an exciting trek!

Day 1: Arrive Mcleodganj | Local Sightseeing

Explore the beautiful town of Mcleodganj. Visit Dalai Lama Temple, Bhagsu Waterfall, and experience Tibetan culture.

Day 2: Triund Trek

Trek to Triund at 9,350 ft. Enjoy stunning views of Dhauladhar ranges and camp under the starlit sky.

Day 3: Trek Down | Dharamshala Sightseeing | Depart for Delhi

Descend from Triund, explore Dharamshala, and begin your journey back to Delhi with unforgettable memories.
