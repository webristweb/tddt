About Chopta Tungnath Chandrashila

Location: Uttarakhand
Trek Duration: 2 Days / 1 Night
Best Time: April - June / Sept - Late Night / Early Morning
Transport: Depending on group size

The Chopta-Chandrashila Trek is one of the most scenic and rewarding Himalayan treks in Uttarakhand, perfect for beginners as well as experienced trekkers. Starting from Chopta, often called the "Mini Switzerland of India," the trail takes you through dense forests of rhododendron and oak, leading to the ancient Tungnath Temple – the highest Shiva temple in the world at 12,073 feet.

From Tungnath, the trek continues to the Chandrashila Summit, perched at an altitude of around 4,000 meters (13,188 ft). The summit offers breathtaking 360-degree views of some of the tallest Himalayan peaks, including Nanda Devi, Trishul, Chaukhamba, and Kedar Peak. This trek is known for its short duration, well-marked trails, and ever-changing landscapes – snow-covered paths in winter, blooming rhododendrons in spring, and crystal-clear skies in autumn. It is ideal for those seeking a blend of adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty in a compressed timeframe.

DAY 0: DEPARTURE FROM DELHI

Begin your spiritual and adventurous journey from Delhi with an overnight travel to Chopta, the "Mini Switzerland of India." Travel through scenic mountain roads as you head towards the Garhwal Himalayas. Get ready to experience one of the most beautiful and accessible Himalayan treks.

DAY 1: DEVRIYATAL SANGAM - DEORIATAL TREK - OVERNIGHT IN SARI

Arrive at Sari village and trek to the pristine Deoriatal Lake, a crystal-clear high-altitude lake offering stunning reflections of Chaukhamba peaks. The 2.5 km trek through dense forests is perfect for acclimatization. Enjoy the serene beauty of the lake surrounded by lush greenery and snow-capped mountains. Overnight stay in Sari village with campfire and stargazing.

DAY 2: TUNGNATH - CHANDRASHILA TREK - RETURN TO DELHI

Start early morning trek from Chopta to Tungnath Temple (3.5 km), the highest Shiva temple in the world at 12,073 feet. Walk through enchanting rhododendron forests and meadows. After seeking blessings at Tungnath, continue the steep 1.5 km trek to Chandrashila Summit at 13,188 feet. Witness breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views of Nanda Devi, Trishul, Chaukhamba, Kedar Peak, and countless other Himalayan giants. Descend back to Chopta and begin your return journey to Delhi with unforgettable memories.
