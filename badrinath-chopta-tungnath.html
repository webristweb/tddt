About Badrinath With Chopta Tungnath

Location: Uttarakhand
Trek Duration: 4 Days
Difficulty Level: Easy to Moderate

Badrinath with Chopta-Tungnath Trek - This spiritually enriching journey takes you through some of the most sacred and breathtaking landscapes of Uttarakhand. Visit Badrinath, one of the revered Char Dham pilgrimage sites, nestled along the banks of the Alaknanda River and surrounded by majestic Himalayan peaks. Continue towards Chopta, often called the "Mini Switzerland of India," where lush alpine forests create a serene atmosphere.

Trek to Tungnath Temple, the highest Shiva temple in the world at 12,073 feet, and further to Chandrashila Summit for panoramic 360-degree views of the Himalayas. This perfect blend of spirituality, adventure, and natural beauty offers an unforgettable Himalayan experience.

DAY 0: DEPARTURE FROM DELHI

Begin your spiritual and adventurous journey from Delhi with an overnight travel to the sacred lands of Uttarakhand. Get ready to experience the divine energy of Badrinath and the pristine beauty of Chopta-Tungnath. Travel through scenic mountain roads as you head towards the Garhwal Himalayas.

DAY 1: HARIDWAR TO BADRINATH

Drive from Haridwar through spectacular mountain roads, passing through Devprayag (confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi), Rudraprayag, and Joshimath. Reach Badrinath, one of the four sacred Char Dham sites at 10,279 feet. Visit the ancient Badrinath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, situated on the banks of the Alaknanda River. Explore Mana Village (last Indian village before Tibet border), Vyas Gufa, and Bhim Pul. Experience the spiritual aura and divine atmosphere. Overnight stay in Badrinath.

DAY 2: BADRINATH TO CHOPTA

After morning darshan at Badrinath Temple, drive to Chopta, the "Mini Switzerland of India." Journey through scenic valleys, dense forests, and picturesque mountain landscapes. Reach Chopta, a serene meadow surrounded by rhododendron and deodar forests. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and stunning views of Himalayan peaks. Overnight stay in Chopta.

DAY 3: CHOPTA TO TUNGNATH TREK

Early morning trek from Chopta to Tungnath Temple (3.5 km), the highest Shiva temple in the world at 12,073 feet. Walk through enchanting rhododendron forests and alpine meadows with stunning mountain views. After seeking blessings at Tungnath, continue the steep 1.5 km trek to Chandrashila Summit at 13,188 feet. Witness breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views of Nanda Devi, Trishul, Chaukhamba, Kedar Peak, and countless other Himalayan giants. Descend back to Chopta. Overnight stay in Chopta.

DAY 4: DEORIATAL LAKE TREK AND RETURN TO DELHI

Optional early morning trek to the pristine Deoriatal Lake, offering stunning reflections of Chaukhamba peaks. Drive back to Delhi through scenic mountain roads. Reflect on the incredible spiritual journey to Badrinath, the trek to the highest Shiva temple at Tungnath, and the majestic views from Chandrashila Summit. Arrive in Delhi with divine blessings and unforgettable memories.
