About KANATAL – TEHRI LAKE – RISHIKESH

Location: Tehri Garhwal, Uttarakhand
Tour Duration: 2D+1N
Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult

Kanatal and Tehri Lake offer a serene escape in the lap of the Garhwal Himalayas, where nature's beauty meets adventure and peace. Explore the picturesque landscapes of Kanatal, a quaint hill station known for its apple orchards, scenic vistas, and tranquil atmosphere. Witness the breathtaking Tehri Lake, one of Asia's largest man-made reservoirs, perfect spot for water sports and relaxation. This package promises a rejuvenating experience close to nature, with stunning views and unforgettable memories.

DAY 0: Departure from Delhi

Begin your journey from Delhi with an overnight travel to the serene landscapes of Uttarakhand. Get ready for an adventure filled with natural beauty and tranquility.

DAY 1: Tehri Lake – Kanatal Stay & Local Exploration

Arrive at the magnificent Tehri Lake, one of Asia's largest artificial reservoirs. Enjoy water activities or simply soak in the pristine views. Later, head to Kanatal for a peaceful stay amidst pine forests and apple orchards. Explore the local area and enjoy the serene mountain atmosphere.

DAY 2: Surkanda Devi Temple – Rishikesh – Delhi Return

Start your day with a visit to the sacred Surkanda Devi Temple, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas. Continue your journey to Rishikesh, the yoga capital of the world. Explore the iconic Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, and attend the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. Depart for Delhi with cherished memories of this spiritual and scenic journey.
