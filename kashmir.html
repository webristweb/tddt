Trek Shrek – Kashmir Itinerary

Location: Jammu & Kashmir
Tour Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights
Pickup & Drop: Delhi to Delhi

Experience the ethereal beauty of Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth." This comprehensive tour takes you through the stunning valleys, pristine lakes, snow-capped mountains, and Mughal gardens of Kashmir. From the serene Dal Lake with its iconic houseboats to the meadows of Gulmarg and Pahalgam, from the saffron fields to ancient temples - Kashmir offers an unforgettable journey through nature's masterpiece.

Explore Srinagar's Mughal gardens, enjoy shikara rides on Dal Lake, experience the adventure activities in Gulmarg, discover the beauty of Pahalgam's valleys, and witness the breathtaking landscapes of Sonmarg. This tour promises a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, culture, and tranquility.

DAY 0 – DEPARTURE FROM DELHI TO JAMMU

Begin your journey to "Paradise on Earth" with an overnight travel from Delhi to Jammu. Get ready to experience the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir - its pristine valleys, serene lakes, snow-capped mountains, and warm hospitality. Travel through scenic routes as you head towards the enchanting Kashmir Valley.

DAY 1 – TRANSFER FROM JAMMU TO PAHALGAM

Arrive in Jammu and drive to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds," through scenic mountain roads. Pass through saffron fields and traditional Kashmiri villages. Reach Pahalgam, situated at the confluence of Lidder River and Sheshnag Lake streams. Explore the beautiful Betaab Valley (named after Bollywood movie Betaab), Aru Valley, and Chandanwari. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and stunning mountain views. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

DAY 2 – PAHALGAM TO SRINAGAR

Drive from Pahalgam to Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir. Check into a traditional houseboat on Dal Lake or hotel. Enjoy a relaxing shikara ride on the iconic Dal Lake, passing through floating gardens and markets. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens - Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahi. Explore the local markets and experience Kashmiri culture. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

DAY 3 – GULMARG SIGHTSEEING

Day trip to Gulmarg, the "Meadow of Flowers," one of Asia's premier ski resorts. Take the famous Gulmarg Gondola cable car ride (one of the highest in the world) to Apharwat Peak for breathtaking panoramic views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks. Enjoy activities like skiing (in winter), snowboarding, or simply walk through the beautiful meadows. Visit St. Mary's Church and enjoy the stunning landscape. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 4 – SONMARG SIGHTSEEING

Day trip to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold," located at 9,000 feet. Drive through scenic valleys along the Sindh River. Enjoy pony rides to Thajiwas Glacier (optional), surrounded by snow-capped peaks even in summer. Experience the pristine beauty of alpine meadows, glaciers, and crystal-clear streams. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 5 – SRINAGAR SIGHTSEEING

Explore Srinagar city. Visit the historic Shankaracharya Temple perched on a hilltop with panoramic views of Srinagar. Explore Hazratbal Shrine, Jama Masjid, and the old city. Shop for authentic Kashmiri handicrafts, Pashmina shawls, carpets, and dry fruits at Lal Chowk and local markets. Enjoy another shikara ride on Dal Lake during sunset. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 6 – SRINAGAR TO JAMMU

After breakfast, drive back to Jammu through the scenic Kashmir Valley. Enjoy the last glimpses of the beautiful landscapes, valleys, and mountains. Reach Jammu and prepare for your return journey. Overnight stay in Jammu or board the train/bus to Delhi.

Day 7: REACH TO DELHI

Arrive back in Delhi with unforgettable memories of Kashmir - the serene Dal Lake, majestic mountains, beautiful valleys, Mughal gardens, and the warm Kashmiri hospitality. Carry home the essence of "Paradise on Earth."
