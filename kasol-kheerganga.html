About Kasol Kheerganga

Location: Himachal Pradesh
Trek Duration: 3 Days
Difficulty Level: Easy
Trekking distance: 12km
Age limit: 12 to 60 Years

Trek to Kheerganga, a serene Himalayan meadow nestled in Parvati Valley. Soak in the natural hot water springs at Kheerganga amidst panoramic mountain views. Explore Kasol – the backpacker's paradise with rusty cafes, flea markets & stunning views. Relax beside Gurushikhar, Kheerganga's sacred Shiva temple. Sit back on this adventurous trek with the assistance of a friendly and experienced guide.

DAY 0: DEPARTURE TO KASOL OVERNIGHT JOURNEY

Begin your adventure with an overnight journey to Kasol. Get ready for an exciting trek to the beautiful Parvati Valley!

DAY 1: KASOL | MANIKARAN | CAFE HOPIN

Explore the charming village of Kasol, visit the holy Manikaran Gurudwara with its hot springs, and enjoy cafe hopping in the hippie paradise.

DAY 2: KHEERGANGA TREK

Trek through pine forests and scenic landscapes to reach Kheerganga. Relax in natural hot water springs surrounded by majestic mountains.

DAY 3: TREK DOWN | RETURN BACK TO DELHI

Descend from Kheerganga and begin your journey back to Delhi with unforgettable memories of the Himalayas.
