MANALI
================================================================================

TRIP TITLE: MANALI ADVENTURE
SUBTITLE: Rohtang • Kasol • Manikaran • Hidimba Temple • Jogini Waterfall

QUICK INFO:
  Duration: 4 Days
  Starting From: ₹5,999
  Location: Himachal
  Trip Type: Group Tour

DETAILED ITINERARY:

Day 1: Delhi to Manali
  - Departure from Delhi at 08:00 PM in AC Vehicle
  - Take Dinner/Breakfast during travel
  - Overnight journey to Manali
  - Enjoy your travel with your friends
  - Carry light blanket for night journey

Day 2: Manali Sightseeing
  - Reach Manali in morning/noon
  - Check-in to Hotel and take rest
  - Freshen up, get ready and take breakfast
  - Visit Jogini Waterfall trek (about 3 km)
  - Visit Hidimba Devi Temple & Mall Road for shopping
  - Back to hotel by evening. Take rest and dinner at hotel
  - Overnight stay at hotel in Manali

Day 3: Snow Point - Rohtang/Sissu
  - Wake up, freshen up and get ready
  - Breakfast will be served at hotel
  - Leave for Snow Point - Rohtang Pass/Sissu Valley via Atal Tunnel
  - Reach Sissu Valley. Enjoy in snow - You can do some self-paid snow activities in Sissu/Solang Valley
  - Evening come back to hotel & take rest
  - Get ready for craziest DJ Night & Bonfire today
  - Take dinner at hotel. Enjoy DJ Night & Bonfire at hotel
  - Overnight stay at Manali Hotel

Day 4: Manali - Manikaran - Delhi
  - Wake up, freshen up and get ready
  - Breakfast will be served at hotel
  - Check out from hotel and leave for Manikaran
  - Manikaran Gurudwara is the world famous Gurudwara for its natural hot spring water
  - Reach Manikaran Gurudwara, you can take langgar and explore Manikaran market
  - You can explore Kasol market if time allows
  - Later in evening leave for Delhi
  - Overnight journey to Delhi

Day 5: Reach Delhi
  - Reach Delhi in morning/noon depending on traffic
  - Click last group picture/selfie
  - Say bye to your trip captain and bus driver
  - Leave for home with beautiful memories

INCLUSIONS:
  ? Travelling by AC Bus/Traveller
  ? 2 Night Manali Hotel Stay
  ? All local sightseeing
  ? Sissu Valley & Atal Tunnel Snow Point
  ? Jogini Waterfall Trek
  ? Manikaran Gurudwara Kasol
  ? Meals (3 Breakfast & 2 Dinner)
  ? Best & Crazy DJ Night & Bonfire
  ? Trip coordinator discount on adventurous activities
  ? All toll taxes, parking included

EXCLUSIONS:
  ? Any personal expenses
  ? Anything not mentioned in the itinerary
  ? Any meals/drinks other than inclusions
  ? Any meal during travelling
  ? 5% GST charge
