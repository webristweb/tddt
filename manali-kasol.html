About MANALI KASOL

Location: Himachal Pradesh

The Manali-Kasol trek is a scenic Himalayan journey that connects the lush valleys of Kullu and Parvati. Starting from Manali, the trail passes through dense pine forests, alpine meadows, river crossings, and remote mountain villages before reaching Kasol. This trek offers stunning views of snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear rivers, and the serene Parvati Valley. It's perfect for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for a peaceful mountain escape.

DAY 0: DELHI- MANALI

Begin your Himalayan adventure with an overnight journey from Delhi to Manali. Travel through scenic mountain roads as you head towards the beautiful Kullu Valley. Get ready to experience the charm of Himachal Pradesh's most popular hill stations and the serene Parvati Valley.

DAY 1: MANALI SIGHTSEEING

Explore the enchanting town of Manali. Visit the ancient Hadimba Temple surrounded by cedar forests, stroll through Mall Road for shopping and local cuisine, explore Old Manali's hippie cafes and vibrant culture. Visit Vashisht hot springs and temple, enjoy panoramic views from Solang Valley, and experience adventure activities like paragliding and zorbing. Overnight stay in Manali.

DAY 2: SNOW POINT - ROHTANG-SISSU

Drive to the famous Rohtang Pass at 13,050 feet (subject to permit availability). Experience snow activities and breathtaking views of glaciers and peaks. Continue to Sissu, a picturesque village in Lahaul Valley with stunning waterfalls and mountain scenery. Enjoy the dramatic landscape change from lush green valleys to barren mountains. Overnight stay in Sissu.

DAY 3: MANALI – ACTIVITIES - KASOL

Drive from Manali to Kasol through the scenic Parvati Valley. En route, stop at Manikaran to visit the holy Gurudwara with natural hot springs. Arrive in Kasol, the backpacker's paradise known for its Israeli cafes, flea markets, and laid-back vibe. Explore the village, enjoy riverside walks along the Parvati River, and experience cafe hopping. Overnight stay in Kasol.

DAY 4: MANIKARAN - DELHI

Visit Manikaran Gurudwara and its famous hot springs in the morning. Take a dip in the natural hot water springs believed to have healing properties. Explore the local market and enjoy the spiritual atmosphere. Begin your return journey to Delhi with unforgettable memories of Manali, Kasol, and the beautiful Himalayan valleys.

DAY 5: REACH DELHI

Arrive back in Delhi with cherished memories of the scenic Manali-Kasol journey, adventure activities, spiritual experiences, and the serene beauty of Himachal Pradesh.
