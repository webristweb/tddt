About Chakrata Tigerfalls

Location: Uttarakhand
Trek Duration: 2 days + 1 Night
Difficulty Level: Easy
Max Altitude: 6,948 Ft (2,118 meters)

Chakrata, a serene hill station nestled at 7,000 ft in Dehradun, is a haven for peace and nature lovers. Explore the lush meadows of Moila Top with its ancient temples, uncover the mysteries of Budher Caves, and witness nature spirits you with refreshing joy.

DAY 0: DEPARTURE TO DEHRADUN OVERNIGHT JOURNEY

Begin your adventure with an overnight journey to Dehradun. Get ready for an exciting trek ahead!

DAY 1: MOILA | BUDYAL | CHILMIRI

Explore the beautiful meadows of Moila, visit ancient temples at Budyal, and experience the serene environment of Chilmiri.

DAY 2: TIGER FALL

Visit the magnificent Tiger Falls, one of the highest waterfalls in Uttarakhand. Enjoy the breathtaking views and natural beauty.
