About Chitkul Kalpa Trek

Location: Himachal Pradesh
Trek Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights

Chitkul-Kalpa Trek - Located in the scenic Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh, Chitkul and Kalpa offer a perfect blend of raw Himalayan beauty and cultural charm. Chitkul, the last inhabited village near the Indo-Tibet border, is known for its pristine beauty and traditional wooden houses. Kalpa, perched on the Sutlej River banks, offers stunning views of the majestic Kinner Kailash range.

Experience the untouched beauty of these remote villages, walk through apple orchards, witness ancient Buddhist monasteries, and immerse yourself in the rich Kinnauri culture. This journey promises breathtaking landscapes, serene atmosphere, and an authentic Himalayan experience.

DAY 0: DEPART FROM DELHI – OVERNIGHT

Begin your journey to the remote and beautiful Kinnaur region with an overnight travel from Delhi. Get ready to explore the last inhabited villages near the Indo-Tibet border and experience the raw beauty of the Himalayas. Travel through scenic mountain roads as you head towards Himachal Pradesh.

DAY 1: CHITKUL ARRIVAL VIA SHIMLA – NARKANDA

Drive through Shimla, the summer capital of British India, and continue through Narkanda with its apple orchards and pine forests. Journey along the Sutlej River valley through spectacular mountain landscapes. Reach Chitkul, the last inhabited village near the Indo-Tibet border at 11,319 feet. Explore this pristine village with traditional wooden houses, the ancient Mathi Temple, and stunning views of snow-capped peaks. Walk along the Baspa River and experience the serene atmosphere. Overnight stay in Chitkul.

DAY 2: CHITKUL EXPLORE

Spend the full day exploring the beautiful Chitkul village and its surroundings. Visit the ancient Mathi Temple dedicated to the local deity. Walk through apple orchards and traditional Kinnauri houses with intricate wooden architecture. Trek to nearby viewpoints for panoramic views of the Baspa Valley and surrounding peaks. Interact with friendly locals and learn about Kinnauri culture and traditions. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of this remote Himalayan village. Overnight stay in Chitkul.

DAY 3: KALPA ARRIVAL VIA SANGLA VALLEY

Drive through the stunning Sangla Valley, known for its scenic beauty and traditional villages. Pass through Sangla town and continue to Kalpa, a picturesque village perched at 9,711 feet. Kalpa offers breathtaking views of the majestic Kinner Kailash range (6,050m). Visit the ancient Hu-Bu-Lan-Kar Monastery and explore the traditional Kinnauri architecture. Walk through apple orchards and enjoy the serene mountain atmosphere. Witness the stunning sunset views over Kinner Kailash. Overnight stay in Kalpa.

DAY 4: EXPLORE KALPA AND DEPART FOR DELHI

Early morning, witness the spectacular sunrise over Kinner Kailash range with changing colors on the snow-covered peaks. Explore Kalpa village, visit local temples and monasteries. Walk through apple orchards and interact with locals. Begin your return journey to Delhi with unforgettable memories of Chitkul and Kalpa, the pristine beauty of Kinnaur, and the warm hospitality of the Kinnauri people.
